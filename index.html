Arc Shot

A shot in which the subject is photographed by an encircling or moving camera

Interlude

A brife, intervening film scene or sequence, not specifically tied to the plot, that appears within a film

Prologe

A speech, preface, intoduction, or brief scene preceeding the main action or polt of a film: contrast to epilogue
